The Problem
  • The Gap: Ecommerce brands outside of Amazon have creators and video players, but lack the "engine" to scale creator UGC for catalog-wide conversion optimization.
  • The Friction: Traditional UGC acquisition struggles to scale due to upfront valuation, manual efforts, and over reliance on product gifting/seeding incentives.
  • The Developer Bottleneck: Large brands face year-long builds for custom product seeding, advanced attribution tracking, and creator payout systems, straining their developer resources.
The first AI-powered onsite creator incrementality engine, bridging the gap between social commerce and the checkout button.
The Solution
  • Provide the "Missing Link": A platform that pays creators based on the true incremental lift that their UGC videos deliver at checkout.
  • Make it Easy to Integrate: Allow brands to launch in weeks, not months, by connecting with their existing creator and e-commerce stacks without requiring them to write new code.
  • Power it with AI Agents: Automate the entire creator UGC lifecycle from acquisition through approval, syndication, engagement tracking, sales attribution, and creator commission payouts.
Why Now
  • Content Utilization Gap: Brands are sitting on massive libraries of passive UGC video because less than 10% of their social content drives meaningful social commerce results due to referral "link-click" friction.
  • Affiliate Platforms: Legacy affiliate tech is totally dependent on a referral link-click, and does not work natively for tracking creator onsite video engagement and sales attribution.
  • UGC Platforms: Pushing UGC creators out of platform and agency closed networks and into direct-to-brand performance deals is a demise of their business model.

How We Guarantee Incrementality
Most platforms charge for sales that would have happened anyway. We don't.
  • "Try-Before-You-Buy" Program: Customers onboard to measure ROI before they ever recruit a creator using an onsite commission offer. This isolates the exact sales lift from a statistically valid sample of onsite creator UGC placements.
  • Customizable Attribution: Customers use sales lift data from this test to implement the optimal commission structure through customizable settings for attribution model, attribution window and SKU level margins.

How Our Platform Works
Customers install our SDK to send video playback exposures and checkout transactions to our Onsite Commission Engine.
  1. 1. Our AI Agent invites their creator community to join their onsite commission dashboard.
  2. 2. Creators upload UGC videos and track approval status, playbacks and commissions.
  3. 3. Our AI Agent approves & traffics the video to the brand's onsite video player.
  4. 4. We calculate onsite commissions based on engagements, transactions, and custom attribution settings.
  5. 5. Commissions are validated and automatically paid out to creators.
Trust & Safety
Our Onsite Commission Engine includes built-in fraud controls and brand safety features.
  • Creator & Content Safety: Vetting powered by AI and real humans
  • Bot Suppression: Bot playback suppression to prevent fraudulent views
  • Purchase Monitoring: Ensuring creators are not buying their own products to earn commissions
  • Content Protection: Built-in AI to prevent content duplication, stolen UGC, and IP infringement
  • Commission Validation: Process to account for order returns, refunds and cancellations
